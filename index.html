About Tilly

Tilly is an average puppy who lives in an average house living an average life. OR IS SHE....Tilly is the sort of dog that goes crazy when you see people, loves cuddles and above all loves and i mean Loves licking people.Tilly has been everywhere from house to parks BUT... her favourite place is the backyard.In the backyard she plays, runs and jumps.In the house she becomes the nicest puppy you could EVER meet.She sits on you cuddles you and supports you when you need it. She is the best, funniest and best puppy i could ever ask for.
